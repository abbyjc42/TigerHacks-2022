METRO CARDS ARE NO LONGER ACCEPTED

The OMNY system was rolled out in 2019. In 2024, the OMNY system will completely replace the Metro Card system.

OMNY currently has 3 options: Tap to Pay, Microchip Cards, or an OMNY card.


TAP TO PAY

Tap to Pay options like Apple and Google Pay on Smartphones and Smartwatches can be used at the turnstiles. Just use the tap to pay like you would normally at a kiosk until the green check appears.

MICROCHIP CARDS

Micro Chip Credit or Debit Cards, like ones issued through Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Discover, JCB, and UnionPay can be used at OMNY turnstiles. Just tap the card against the Turnstile until the Green check appers.

PHYSICAL OMNY CARDS CAN BE PURCHASED AT RETAILERS

Physical OMNY Cards are available for purchase at these locations. Cards are $5 before fares and can be reloaded at these retailers or online if the OMNY Card is registered. To use at the turnstiles, just tap the card until the green check appears.
